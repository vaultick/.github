## 📜 Manifesto

Vaultick is an infrastructure-as-code project dedicated to the long-term preservation of digital secrets. We believe that:
1.  **Encryption should happen at the edge (the browser),** never on the server.
2.  **One-time payment. Long-term control.** (No subscriptions)
3.  **Code implies trust.** We open-source our cryptography implementation so you don't have to take our word for it.
